# **App Name**: Eid Count

## Core Features:

- Quick Add Buttons: Pre-set buttons for common cash amounts (5, 10, 50, 100, 500) for easy tapping and adding.
- Total Display: Clearly shows the current total amount of money collected.
- Add Custom Amount: An input field to add any custom cash amount not covered by quick add buttons.
- Undo Last Entry: A button to undo the last money entry in case of a mistake.
- Local Storage Persistence: Automatically saves the current total to the browser's local memory so it's not lost on refresh or closing the app.
- Congratulatory Messages (AI): A generative AI tool that provides fun, encouraging messages and virtual 'stickers' to the child as they reach certain money milestones or after adding new amounts, celebrating their progress.
- Reset Functionality: Option to reset the total count and start over, with a confirmation to prevent accidental data loss.

## Style Guidelines:

- Color scheme: Light and celebratory. Primary: A rich saffron yellow (#FFBF00) evoking festivity and precious metals. Background: A soft, warm off-white with a hint of yellow (#FDFBED) to maintain a bright, clean feel. Accent: A vibrant coral orange (#FF6B6B) to highlight interactive elements and provide playful contrast.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans' (sans-serif) for its friendly, modern readability and a touch of warmth suitable for a family app. It works well for both numbers and text.
- Use simple, clean line icons. For addition, money-related, and celebratory themes. For example, a plus sign, a money bag, or a celebratory spark.
- Mobile-first, single-column layout optimized for small screens. Large, tactile buttons for money entry to ensure ease of use for small hands and on-the-go interaction. Clear, oversized display for the total amount.
- Subtle, satisfying 'bounce' or 'pop' animations on button presses and a gentle upward 'counting' animation when the total sum increases, providing immediate feedback and a sense of progress.
